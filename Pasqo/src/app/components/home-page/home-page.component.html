PASQO





What it is

Pasqo is an online quiz-practice platform created by a student for students.
It provides an easier and efficient way for students to prepare for quizzes and examinations.



Aims

It aims to solve two major problems
~ Students not having a quick and easy access to past questions
~ Students not having an easier way of getting answers for these past questions

HOW IT WORKS

~ LEARN ~


Solving past questions to prepare for an exam or quiz is a great hack that most students use. But for some subjects it's not going to be enough, so we recommend that you learn your slides or books, and get some understanding of the subject matter. Then you can go on to test your knowledge with past questions.

~ SOLVE PASQO ~


Traditionally, you'd have to either go to the library and comb through stacks of past questions just to find the particular one you're looking for, or ask friends and mates whom usually are going through the same hell as you are.
Learning is hard enough as it is already, not to add the burden of searching for past questions. Well, now with PASQO's vast database of past questions with their answers, you don't have to go through that anymore. All you need is a computer or phone, and an internet connection. You can study past questions on the go.

~ TAKE QUIZ ~


Have you ever seen "shacks" walk into a examination hall feeling so confident??? Wish you could go take an exam with the same confidence, and not have to rely on others???
Well, good thing is you don't have to be a genius to pass an exam. You've studied, you've solved some PASQO, now go show that quiz who's BOSS!

~ PASS WITH FLYING COLORS ~


One of the most basic principles in life is

- you reap what you sow -

This short but powerful priniciple is true for every aspect of life, so if you've put in the work and time to study, solve some PASQO, and prepare for an exam, then you should sit back, relax, and expect nothing but great results.
It's as simple as that!
