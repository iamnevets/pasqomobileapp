What we do


We provide students with an easier, faster, and modern way of studying past questions for a quiz. Our remote developer-team works hard to maintain the platform, develop new features in order to improve and make it as interactive and user-friendly as possible.
Our awesome campus reps(admins) work tirelessly to get their hands on any past question from their assigned courses, get the correct answers for the questions from Lecturers or get some students to solve them, and then finally type and upload the questions unto the platform.

How we work


These days thanks to the internet, people can stay connected from different locations, far or near. Our team is spread across different campuses all over the nation, so we work remotely and have scheduled meetings online. We also have a whatsapp group where the team interacts and stays in touch with one another.

Our workflow is quite simple:
Develop new features, fix bugs, provide support, and add more past questions.
